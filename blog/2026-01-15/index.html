    1. OpenAI Invests in Merge Labs
      OpenAI is supporting Merge Labs with an investment geared towards developing brain-computer interfaces that enhance human capabilities by integrating biological and artificial intelligence. More details can be found in the OpenAI Blog.

    2. Strengthening U.S. AI Supply Chain
      OpenAI has initiated a request for proposals aimed at bolstering the U.S. AI supply chain, emphasizing domestic manufacturing to bolster jobs and infrastructure. More information is available in this OpenAI article.

    3. AI Medical Diagnostics Race Intensifies
      OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic have all unveiled competing medical AI diagnostic tools recently, raising questions about their readiness for clinical application. Further details can be found in this piece on AI News.

    4. NVIDIA Open-Sources KVzap
      NVIDIA has released KVzap, a cutting-edge method for efficient key-value (KV) cache pruning in transformer models, which can lead to dramatic reductions in memory usage. Read more on MarkTechPost.

    5. AI Chatbot in Recruitment by McKinsey
      McKinsey is testing an AI chatbot to assist in the initial phases of graduate recruitment, hinting at a transformative approach in talent acquisition. For more, see AI News.

    • Toward Ultra-Long-Horizon Agentic Science: Cognitive Accumulation for Machine Learning Engineering
      This study introduces a framework for autonomous agents that enhances long-term strategic coherence in engineering tasks, using cognitive accumulation techniques.

    • SatMap: Revisiting Satellite Maps as Prior for Online HD Map Construction
      A new method for online HD map estimation that leverages satellite imagery with camera observations, improving accuracy and robustness.

    Robotics and Control

    • FlowAct-R1: Towards Interactive Humanoid Video Generation
      Introduces a real-time system for generating humanoid videos that combines low-latency responsiveness with fine-grained control over characters' behaviors.

    • CoF-T2I: Video Models as Pure Visual Reasoners for Text-to-Image Generation
      A novel model that seamlessly integrates video reasoning into text-to-image generation, improving performance over traditional models.

    • ReasAlign: Reasoning Enhanced Safety Alignment against Prompt Injection Attack
      This work presents a model-level solution designed to improve safety alignment against indirect prompt injection attacks through structured reasoning steps.
